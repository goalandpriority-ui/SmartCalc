Before You Contact Us

Many common questions are already answered below. Check if your question is covered here before sending us an email — it is often faster!

Are all the calculators on CalculatorHub free?

Yes, 100%. Every single calculator on CalculatorHub is completely free to use. There is no subscription, no account required, and no hidden fees of any kind. We are supported by non-intrusive advertising.

How accurate are your calculations?

We use verified mathematical formulas from authoritative sources including the Reserve Bank of India (RBI), World Health Organization (WHO), Indian Income Tax Department, and US IRS guidelines. All formulas are reviewed regularly for accuracy. That said, results are for educational purposes and should not replace advice from a qualified professional.

Do you store my data when I use a calculator?

No. All calculations happen locally in your browser using JavaScript. We do not transmit, collect, or store any values you enter into our calculators. Your financial and personal data is completely private.

I found a calculation error. How do I report it?

We appreciate bug reports! Please email us at support@calculatorhub.website with the calculator name, the values you entered, and the result you expected. We typically fix reported errors within 24–48 hours.

Can I request a new calculator?

Absolutely! We regularly add new calculators based on user requests. Email us your idea with a brief description of what the calculator should do, and we will evaluate it for our next update. We have already built several calculators based on user suggestions.

Can I embed your calculator on my website?

Please contact us at support@calculatorhub.website to discuss embedding options. We may be able to arrange this on a case-by-case basis. Unauthorized embedding or copying of our calculator code is not permitted under our Terms of Use.

Do you offer a mobile app?

Not yet, but CalculatorHub is fully optimized for mobile browsers and works like a native app. You can add it to your home screen from your browser's menu for a near-app experience. A dedicated mobile app is on our roadmap.

What to Include in Your Message

To help us respond quickly and accurately, please include the following when contacting us:

  • Type of inquiry — Bug report, feature request, general question, business inquiry, etc.
  • Calculator name — Which specific calculator are you referring to?
  • Device and browser — This helps us reproduce and fix bugs faster
  • Description — A clear description of your question, issue, or idea
  • Expected vs actual result — For bug reports, tell us what result you expected vs what you got
