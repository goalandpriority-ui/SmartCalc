1. Overview

Welcome to CalculatorHub ("we," "us," or "our"), accessible at calculatorhub.website. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe, trustworthy experience on our platform.

This Privacy Policy describes what information we collect when you visit CalculatorHub, how we use that information, and your choices regarding your data. By using our website, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

CalculatorHub is a free online calculator platform offering 80+ tools for finance, health, education, real estate, business, and utility purposes. Our core service — the calculators themselves — requires no personal information and stores nothing on our servers.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

CalculatorHub does not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use our calculators. If you contact us by email (at support@calculatorhub.website), we will receive:

  • Your email address
  • Your name (if you include it)
  • The content of your message

We use this information only to respond to your inquiry and will not use it for marketing or share it with third parties.

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit CalculatorHub, certain information is collected automatically by our analytics and advertising partners (Google). This includes:

  • Your approximate geographic location (country/city level, not precise address)
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type (mobile, tablet, desktop)
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website (how you arrived at CalculatorHub)
  • IP address (anonymized by Google Analytics)

This information is collected in aggregate, anonymized form and is used to understand how visitors use our website so we can improve it.

3. Calculator Data & Your Privacy

✅ Your calculator inputs are completely private. All calculations on CalculatorHub run entirely within your web browser using JavaScript. The numbers you enter — your income, loan amount, weight, or any other values — are never sent to our servers, never stored in a database, and never accessible to us or any third party.

When you use our EMI calculator and enter your loan amount, when you use our BMI calculator and enter your weight, or when you use our income tax calculator and enter your salary — that data exists only in your browser's memory and is discarded the moment you close or refresh the page.

We designed CalculatorHub this way intentionally. Financial and health data is sensitive and personal. We believe users should be able to make calculations without worrying about their data being collected, stored, or misused.

4. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand how visitors use CalculatorHub. Google Analytics helps us answer questions like: Which calculators are most popular? Do users primarily visit on mobile or desktop? Which countries do our users come from?

Google Analytics collects anonymized usage data using cookies and similar technologies. Key points:

  • We have enabled IP anonymization, so your full IP address is never stored
  • We do not use Google Analytics to collect personal identification information
  • Data is aggregated — we see trends across all users, not individual behavior
  • You can opt out of Google Analytics using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on

For more information on how Google handles analytics data, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Google AdSense & Advertising

CalculatorHub is a free platform supported by advertising through Google AdSense. Google AdSense displays ads on our website to help cover our hosting and development costs, allowing us to keep all calculators free for everyone.

How Google AdSense works:

  • Google may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website and other websites on the internet
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and other sites on the Internet
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings
  • You may also opt out of third-party vendor use of cookies by visiting www.aboutads.info/choices

Our ads.txt file (accessible at calculatorhub.website/ads.txt) lists all authorized digital sellers for our advertising inventory, in accordance with the IAB Tech Lab's Authorized Digital Sellers specification.

We do not control the content of advertisements displayed by Google AdSense. We do not sell, share, or disclose your personal data to advertisers.

6. Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. CalculatorHub uses the following types of cookies:

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for basic website functionality. They do not collect personal information and cannot be turned off.

Analytics Cookies (Google Analytics)

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website. They collect information in an anonymous form, including the number of visitors, where visitors have come from, and the pages they visited. Cookie names include _ga, _gid, and _gat.

Advertising Cookies (Google AdSense)

These cookies are used by Google AdSense to serve personalized advertisements. They may track your browsing activity across websites. You can control these through Google's Ad Settings or your browser's cookie settings.

Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website. Most browsers allow you to:

  • See what cookies are stored and delete them individually
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block cookies from particular sites
  • Block all cookies from being set
  • Delete all cookies when you close your browser

When you click on a link to an external website, you leave CalculatorHub and are subject to the privacy policy of that external site. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policy of any website you visit.

8. Children's Privacy

CalculatorHub is designed for a general audience and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at support@calculatorhub.website. We will promptly delete any such information from our records.

Our calculators related to education (GPA, study hours, reading speed) are designed to be educational tools suitable for students of all ages, but we do not collect any data from these users.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data. Since CalculatorHub collects minimal personal data (only email data from direct contact), your rights primarily apply to any communication data you have shared with us.

For All Users

  • Right to access — You may request information about what personal data (if any) we hold about you
  • Right to deletion — You may request that we delete any personal data we hold about you
  • Right to opt-out — You can opt out of Google Analytics and personalized ads at any time

For EU/EEA Users (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the right to data portability and the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

For California Users (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). We do not sell your personal information. You have the right to know what personal information is collected, to delete personal information, and to opt-out of the sale of personal information (which we do not engage in).

To exercise any of your rights, please contact us at support@calculatorhub.website.
